ScryLibrary

ScryLibrary is a free Hearthstone and Magic: The Gathering article aggregator.
Data is gathered from dozens of content-producing websites and displayed in a filterable and sortable list.
Being able to quickly search through a library of new articles will prove to be useful for casual and competitive players.
Users are encouraged to visit the source websites of the content shown.

Due to the nature of the games (new cards/sets, rotating formats, bans/unbans), only content from the past 90 days is shown.

Have any questions, suggestions, concerns, or business inquiries? Contact us at scrylibrary@gmail.com

Donations are accepted and handled through PayPal. Ads and donations help us keep the website running and improve our service!
